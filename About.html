About Me

Bio

Early Life

I was born in Portland, Oregon, but moved to Prescott, Az when I was 5. My brother, sister, and I were all homeschooled up to High School, when we attended PHS, the only high school in Prescott. Since I was homeschooled, my education was very efficient and I had more time to dedicate to extracurriculars. I learned violin, piano, and trombone from a very young age, and also participated in several sports including soccer, gymnastics, and swimming. Through high school I participated in as many extracurriculars as possible, including Math Club, Chess Club, Rubiks Cube Club, National Honor Society, Marching Band, and Swim Team. As I went into my senior year at PHS, I grew fairly bored of the small town and was very happy to move to Tucson to attend U of A upon graduating.

Recent

After attending U of A for 2 years, where I had studied Electical and Computer Engineering, I grew fatigued with the stress that comes with engineering, and took a few years off to travel the world. I went to Europe, New Zealand, and Japan, and had the opportunity to talk to people from all walks of life. Newly inspired and wanting to make a difference in the world, I decided to go back to college, and became a CS major at NAU!
